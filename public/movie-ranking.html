The Best Movies According to Kelly

Movies I enjoyed within last year and this year

The Princess Switch

This is a Christmas Hollywood movie that has about three parts. A romantic royal movie that entails two princesses that look exactly alike and were switched on a baking competition.

The Love You Give Me

This is a Chinese drama. A love story between a long-lost girlfriend who later became a wife to the young man she once loved and had a son with.

Water and Fire

This is a Turkish romantic movie about two people in love who fought for their love, but the man gave himself up to save his wife and son. This movie is a lovely movie, but at the same time a really sad movie.
